Individual Donations via Give Now

Your support helps us push the boundaries of autonomous flight technology and compete at the highest levels. Every contribution, no matter the size, directly impacts our ability to innovate, learn, and represent SJSU on the global stage.

Why Support Spartan Flight?

  • Fuel Innovation: Your donation directly supports cutting-edge research and development in autonomous aerial systems, helping students gain hands-on experience with real-world engineering challenges.
  • Support STEM Education: Help us provide valuable learning opportunities for the next generation of aerospace engineers, computer scientists, and innovators.
  • Make an Impact: Your contribution helps us compete against 70+ international teams, bringing recognition to SJSU and advancing the field of unmanned aerial systems.
  • Tax-Deductible: All donations through SJSU's Give Now platform are tax-deductible, making your support even more meaningful.

Donor Benefits

As a thank you for your support, all individual donors receive:

  • ✓ Personal Thank You: Receive a personalized thank you message from our team acknowledging your contribution.
  • ✓ Website Recognition: Your name or logo (if desired) displayed on our sponsors page, showing your commitment to supporting innovation.
  • ✓ Progress Newsletter: Exclusive access to our monthly progress newsletter with detailed updates on our projects, milestones, and competition preparation.
