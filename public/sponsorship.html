Individual Donations via Give Now

For smaller and individual donations, we also accept contributions through SJSU's Give Now platform. This option is intended for individual supporters and smaller donations, not corporate, part, or small business sponsorships.

How to Donate via Give Now

  1. Visit the SJSU Give Now donation page
  2. Find "Spartan Flight" on the list of organizations
  3. Select our organization and complete your donation
