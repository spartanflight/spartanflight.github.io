October 2025

First Flight Tests & General Meeting!

Major milestone for Spartan Flight! We completed initial flight tests with our X650 platform and hosted our first general meeting to open the club to more students.

Flight Test Highlights:

  • Successful initial test flights with X650 platform
  • Endurance testing to evaluate flight time
  • Power system testing to determine 6S vs 8S configuration
  • Validated baseline performance before custom builds

General Meeting & Onboarding:

  • First general meeting to open club to more students
  • Presented 2026 SUAS competition goals
  • Started onboarding new members across all teams
  • Began CAD work, schematic design, and software development
  • Started pursuing sponsors and funding opportunities

Now that we have initial baseline and benchmark data, we can start building the competition drone! Until this point, all funding came out of pocket from the founding members.

September 2025

Official Club Status & Team Growth

Major milestone - Spartan Flight is now an officially recognized student organization at SJSU! We secured a faculty advisor and established our social media presence to connect with the campus community.

Key Achievements:

  • Received official club recognition from SJSU
  • Secured faculty advisor for technical guidance
  • Launched Instagram, LinkedIn, and Discord channels
  • Established meeting schedule and team structure
  • Began outreach for sponsorships and funding

Follow us on social media to stay updated on our progress!

Posted by Brando Suarez | Business Team

August 2025

Parts Procurement Complete

All major components have been ordered and received! The team spent the month researching vendors, comparing specifications, and making critical purchasing decisions for our competition vehicle.

Major Components Acquired:

  • Pixhawk 6C flight controller
  • NVIDIA Jetson for computer vision
  • RFD900 telemetry system
  • X650 quadcopter airframe kit
  • Four LiPo battery bricks for power
  • Motors, ESCs, and propulsion components

With parts in hand, we're ready to begin assembly and integration!

Posted by Team Leads | Procurement

July 2025

Team Formation & Initial Planning

The journey begins! A group of passionate SJSU engineering students came together with a shared vision: compete in the Student Unmanned Aerial Systems competition and push the boundaries of autonomous flight.

Foundation Work:

  • Assembled founding team members
  • Researched SUAS competition requirements
  • Developed initial system architecture
  • Created project timeline and milestones
  • Began preliminary design discussions

From concept to reality - Spartan Flight is born!
